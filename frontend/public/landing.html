Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. Swipe and Test modes are free, including a daily quota of AI explanations. PRO lifts the limits and unlocks Mock Interview.

Do I need to sign up?

To try — no. A real question deck opens immediately. To save progress, one click via Telegram or email is enough.

How long does it take to prepare for a Java interview?

It depends on level. Our estimator suggests roughly 150 questions for junior, 320 for middle and 520 for senior at ~15 questions/hour. Most people feel confident in 2–3 weeks of 20 minutes a day.

Is this better than LeetCode for interviews?

Different job. LeetCode trains algorithms; Interview Tinder trains the language and system-design questions real interviews actually ask — with instant explanations and a readiness score so you know when to stop.

Which languages are supported?

Java, Python and TypeScript — each with its own independent question bank.

How does the AI explanation work?

Swipe a question left ("don't know") and get a plain-language breakdown generated by an LLM. Answers are cached so you don't wait.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes — as a Telegram Mini App and as an installable PWA on iOS and Android.

Will this guarantee me a job?

No product can. But consistent practice with feedback is the single most reliable way to raise your interview hit-rate.
